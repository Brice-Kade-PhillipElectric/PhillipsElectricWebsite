About Phillips Electric

For over 40 years, Phillips Electric has proudly served the Durango, Colorado community with reliable, high-quality electrical services. Built on a foundation of integrity, craftsmanship, and customer trust, the company has earned a reputation for delivering dependable solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial projects alike.

With decades of hands-on experience, Phillips Electric combines traditional work ethic with modern expertise to meet today’s electrical needs while staying committed to safety, efficiency, and long-lasting results. Whether it’s a small repair or a large-scale installation, customers can count on Phillips Electric for professional service done right the first time.
